## Frequency:
Bluetooth operates between 2402 and 2480 MHz (79 channels x 1MHz), or 2400 and 2483.5 MHz including guard bands 2 MHz wide at the bottom end and 3.5 MHz wide at the top. 

## Technology:
   -  Bluetooth BR/EDR
